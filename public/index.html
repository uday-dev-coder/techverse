01 — About

About the
Event

A two-day inter-college fest built for the next generation of tech innovators, creators, and builders.

Techverse 2025 is a vibrant, two-day Inter-College Technical fest organized by the BCA students, aimed at fostering innovation, collaboration, and creativity in the field of technology. The primary goal of the event is to bridge the gap between academia and industry, and to empower students by providing them with a platform to showcase their skills, engage with cutting-edge technologies, and connect with like-minded peers and professionals.

The event is designed to inspire the next generation of tech leaders through hands-on experiences, competitions, and expert interactions. Whether you're a coder, designer, filmmaker, or just curious about technology, Techverse has something to spark your passion.
